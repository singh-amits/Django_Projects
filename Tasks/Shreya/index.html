Shreya Tours & Travels is a leading Tours & Travels website company based in Mumbai. We provide integrated travel Experience to expedition enthusiasts across the world!

Travel More. Stress less

Shreya Tours & Travels is a full-service travel company dedicated at providing corporate, leisure, meeting & management services. We provide integrated travel experience to expedition enthusiasts across the world! Our 8 years of experience has earned us a niche in the Travel industry.

Our company provides services in all travel related activities like Airline ticketing, Rail ticketing, Car hire, Hotel booking, Passport & VISA services. With this website we aspire to grow our reach far & beyond, and cater more and more voyagers who are looking for a travel rush in India and abroad.
